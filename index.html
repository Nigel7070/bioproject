Vertebral Anatomy

The vertebra is a fundamental building block of the spinal column, providing structural support and protecting the spinal cord.

  • Vertebral Body: Main weight-bearing structure
  • Vertebral Foramen: Passage for spinal cord
  • Spinous Process: Posterior projection for muscle attachment
  • Transverse Processes: Lateral projections
  • Superior Articular Process: Joint surfaces
  • Pedicles: Connect body to posterior arch
  • Laminae: Form posterior wall of vertebral foramen
